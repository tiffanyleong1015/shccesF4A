Chinese Club

學會宗旨：

中文學會乃本校中學學務組轄下的學術組織，成立於2006年，其宗旨是透過舉辦不同的專題課堂和學校活動，以增加學生對中國文化的認識，培養其學習中華文化的興趣，使她們在文史知識、語文能力、品德情操各方面都有所增進。

學會活動：

為了增進學生對中國文化的認識，培養其學習中國語文的興趣，中文學會一直積極舉辦各類型活動，讓學生寓學習於遊戲。同時，本會也透過讓幹事學生帶領活動，培養其領導能力、責任感、思維組織能力及團體精神。過去，中文學會曾舉辦的活動包括：午間小電台、文化刊出版、元宵燈謎攤位、書法比賽、普通話歌唱比賽等，本會亦曾協助中文科組舉辦中國文化周系列活動。今年，本會將繼續舉辦更多元化的活動，讓學生於校園生活中學習更多中國文化知識。

Mathematics Club

Aim

The Mathematics Club aims to work for the interests of our fellow students. We develop students’ mathematical curiosity and interpersonal relationship skills by conducting member meetings with various topics related to Mathematics. The purpose of the Mathematics Club is to arouse students’ interests in Mathematics so that they can become confident in using mathematics to analyze and solve problems both in school and in real-life situations. Mathematics is a tool to train one’s logical thinking skills. It is also a sword which can solve thousands of problems in various fields in our daily life.

Music Associaiton

Aim

To bond students together with enthusiasm and a shared love and passion for music.

Objectives

  1. Let the students achieve higher standard musical performances
  2. Let the students increase their interest in music.
  3. Strengthen the students on communication and the ability of planning and organising activities.

The School Choir, the School Orchestra, the Percussion Band, and the Handbell Team are all teams under the supervision of the Music Association. All four teams have regular practices and internal meetings among officers. Besides, several interest classes including the newly established Wind band, percussion interest classes, and cello ensemble are under the management of the Music Association as well. The Music Association aims to bond students together with enthusiasm and a shared love and passion for music. With the common goal to achieve high standard musical performances, the four teams have participated in various musical events of every scholastic year like Music Festival, Lunch Concert, Christmas Flash Performance, Christmas Party Performance etc. Students have also taking part in various external events and competitions such as the Inter-School Singing Competition and the MYSO concerts.
